PCOS Detection

Welcome to the PCOS Detection page! Here, you can analyze various personal health parameters to assess potential indicators for Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS).

Health Parameters for PCOS Detection

Understanding the relationship between personal health metrics and PCOS can provide valuable insights for early detection and management. Please refer to the following key health indicators:

  • Age (yrs): The age of the individual in years.
  • Weight (Kg): The weight of the individual in kilograms.
  • Height (Cm): The height of the individual in centimeters.
  • BMI: Body Mass Index calculated from height and weight.
  • Blood Group: The individual's blood group.
  • Pulse rate (bpm): The heart rate measured in beats per minute.
  • RR (breaths/min): Respiratory rate, the number of breaths per minute.
  • Hb (g/dl): Hemoglobin level in grams per deciliter.
  • Cycle (R/I): Regular or irregular menstrual cycle.
  • Cycle length (days): Length of the menstrual cycle in days.
  • Marriage Status (Yrs): Number of years married.
  • Pregnant (Y/N): Pregnancy status (Yes/No).
  • No. of abortions: The number of abortions the individual has had.
  • I beta-HCG (mIU/mL): First beta-Human Chorionic Gonadotropin level in mIU/mL.
  • II beta-HCG (mIU/mL): Second beta-Human Chorionic Gonadotropin level in mIU/mL.
  • FSH (mIU/mL): Follicle-stimulating hormone level in mIU/mL.
  • LH (mIU/mL): Luteinizing hormone level in mIU/mL.
  • FSH/LH: Ratio of FSH to LH levels.
  • Hip (inch): Hip circumference in inches.
  • Waist (inch): Waist circumference in inches.
  • Waist:Hip Ratio: The ratio of waist to hip circumference.
  • TSH (mIU/L): Thyroid-stimulating hormone level in mIU/L.
  • AMH (ng/mL): Anti-MÃ¼llerian hormone level in ng/mL.
  • PRL (ng/mL): Prolactin hormone level in ng/mL.
  • Vit D3 (ng/mL): Vitamin D3 level in ng/mL.
  • PRG (ng/mL): Progesterone hormone level in ng/mL.
  • RBS (mg/dl): Random blood sugar level in mg/dL.
  • Weight gain (Y/N): History of significant weight gain (Yes/No).
  • Hair growth (Y/N): Presence of excess hair growth (Yes/No).
  • Skin darkening (Y/N): Skin darkening in specific areas (Yes/No).
  • Hair loss (Y/N): Hair loss or thinning (Yes/No).
  • Pimples (Y/N): Frequent acne or pimples (Yes/No).
  • Fast food (Y/N): Regular consumption of fast food (Yes/No).
  • Reg. Exercise (Y/N): Engagement in regular physical exercise (Yes/No).
  • BP Systolic (mmHg): Systolic blood pressure in mmHg.
  • BP Diastolic (mmHg): Diastolic blood pressure in mmHg.
  • Follicle No. (L): Number of follicles in the left ovary.
  • Follicle No. (R): Number of follicles in the right ovary.
  • Avg. F size (L) (mm): Average follicle size in the left ovary (in mm).
  • Avg. F size (R) (mm): Average follicle size in the right ovary (in mm).
  • Endometrium (mm): Thickness of the endometrial lining (in mm).

For analysis, please enter the above parameters as comma-separated values.
